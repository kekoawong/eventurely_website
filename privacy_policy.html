Privacy Policy

At Eventurely, your data protection and privacy are of the utmost importance to us. With that in mind, our app only collects data that is essential to product development and usage rates. Additionally, we do not sell your information to third-party vendors.

Any information that is stored for the purpose of product development is used to improve app features. This includes information such as:

  • User groups
  • User claps
  • Group information (creation date, name, description, posts, events, members)
  • Event attendance

Usage rate data is collected to better understand how users utilize the Eventurely app and its different features, helping us improve your experience on the platform and expand Eventurely to different communities. Additionally, this information is anonymized and not attached to your name or user id in order to protect your privacy. Eventurely uses Google Analytics for Firebase in order to collect usage rate data. Usage rate data may include:

  • User logins
  • Time spent on app and different screens
  • Clicks and utilization of certain app features
  • Bug and crash data

With the Beta release of Eventurely, users must consent to allow usage rate collection in order to use app features. However, with the release of Eventurely Premium in the coming months, users will have the option to opt out of any data collection that is not essential to product development in the future.

Finally, if you would like to download a zipped JSON file of your user data or request to have your personal information deleted from the Eventurely database, email eventurely@gmail.com with your name, user id, and request and we will get back to you as soon as possible.
